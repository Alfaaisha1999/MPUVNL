Welcome to

Urja Vikas Nigam Limited

The Corporation has a large technically trained manpower well versed in energy audit studies as well as consultancy studies for total energy management for client industries. We not only conduct energy audits for suggesting ways of saving on conventional power but also do feasibility studies for the uses of non conventional energy in industry make pre-feasibility reports for actually setting up non conventional power projects. We are the only such agency in the country having a full fledged Energy Management Cell-setup exclusively for this purpose. To date energy studies have been completed for over 230 private industries in the state.
